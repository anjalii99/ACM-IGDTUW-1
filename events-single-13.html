Digital Image Processing And Its exciting Applications

Time: 12:30-2 PM, Monday, 19 Mar 2018

Location: Seminar Hall, Admin Block, IGDTUW

After a successful session on Python and it’s Applications with 100+ participants, ACM IGDTUW brings to you another exciting opportunity to let you step up your tech skills with an interactive session on Digital Image Processing And it’s exciting Applications.

SUB TOPICS

  • Intro to Digital Image Processing (DIP)
  • DIP coocepts and their Implementation in MATLAB
  • Develop your own Image Processing application- specific MATLAB programs.
  • Applications of DIP like in photoshop, biometrics, Digital Camera and Movies, Machine Vision, etc.

If you edit or share photos via facebook and insta, you use DIP everyday!

So, gear up yourselves and register on the form!

Deadline for filling the form: Sunday, 18th March, 9 PM

NOTE: Carry your laptops for hands on sessions. Install the trial version of MATLAB in your Laptops.
